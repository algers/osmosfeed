The optimisation and redistribution work brought error rates down, which resolved most of the issues with channel loading, group direct messages, hyperlinks, and Saved items that users were experiencing. At this point, we made the call not to re-enable the Threads view in order to give our recovery efforts more time to restore balance to the system.
